MiTai the Moose

When my family and I chat with stickers on the communication app LINE, the stickers can often generate more confusion than clarity. MiTai the Moose is a sticker set designed to remove these ambiguities. MiTai portrays the ideas and emotions of daily life that are repeatedly expressed in family conversations. Design Process

I started the project going through chat history and collecting the most commonly used phrases and sticker responses. Then, I translated the list into initial facial expressions or gesture drawings. I wanted this sticker pack to be personalized to myself and my family while still appealing to the masses, so I chose the basis of the stickers to be a moose. I chose the moose because my relatives would gift me moose stuffed animals from their travels, so there would be an instant connection. Drafting rough sketches of each moose sticker, I finalized them in Photoshop. Ultimately, this sticker set is designed deliberately for families who use messages as a means to update statuses in the daily life.
