Music of the Age

In building my communication design skills, I created a set of music concert posters designed to be viewed together and separately. In this project, I wanted to create a series of concert posters that was cohesive, emotional, and abstract, all while tapping into the specific sounds of the artists.

Design Process

To start, I listened to the music and created moodboards for each artist. I created initial sketches of poster designs I thought could bring the music together. Because a concert denotes fun and excitement for family and friends, I chose from my initial set of sketches the poster version that was most playful and organic to iterate. I melded the colors that complimented the artists to the design, playing with typography, color and composition.

Final Design

My final design takes from the colorfulness of each individual artist and adds structure to create readability and cohesion among the posters. The final composition is designed so that not only can each poster stand alone, each piece within the poster can stand alone and still be recognizable as well.



Project: Concert Poster Series
Duration: 2 weeks
Role: Visual and Communication
